Loomabelle

Turn simple art into embroidery files — right in your browser.

Helpful notes
  • Best with logo-style art (3–6 colors).
  • Color-change stops are added between blocks for home machines.
  • Use Custom hoop to match your frame.

Brother SE2000 tips

  1. Use 5×7 (130×180mm) hoop or set your custom size.
  2. Tap Process → Download .DST, copy to USB (or use Brother’s app).
  3. On the machine, assign thread colors when prompted between blocks.
